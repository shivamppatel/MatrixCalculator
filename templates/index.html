This web application is a Matrix Calculator that performs elementary operations on matrices, ranging from basic addition and multiplication to calculating determinants. The inspiration for this tool came from the course Matrices and Matrix Calculations, M340L-CS, at the University of Texas at Austin. The course taught a plethora of matrix operations that are valuable for data collection and modification. Through the knowledge gained from the course, this tool was developed as an application.
